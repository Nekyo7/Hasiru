Design and generate a modern web application for a **Farm Equipment Sharing Marketplace** called **FarmShare**.

This platform allows farmers to **list, discover, and rent agricultural machinery** such as tractors, harvesters, irrigation pumps, and seeders within their region.

IMPORTANT DESIGN REQUIREMENTS:

The website must **NOT look like a typical corporate SaaS product**. Avoid startup clichés like pricing tables, subscription tiers, or enterprise marketing sections. The design should feel **earthy, community-driven, visual, and practical for farmers**, similar to Airbnb or marketplace apps but themed around agriculture.

STYLE & VISUAL DIRECTION:

Use an **image-heavy layout** with large real-world photos of farmland, tractors, harvesters, irrigation systems, and farmers using equipment.

Color palette should feel natural and agricultural:

* Soil brown (#3A2E2A)
* Wheat gold (#D9A441)
* Leaf green (#4F7A4A)
* Sky blue (#6FA7D2)
* Warm off-white (#F6F3EA)

Typography:

* Headings: Poppins or Inter
* Body text: Nunito or Open Sans

Include subtle textures inspired by farmland patterns or plowed field lines.

Use rounded cards, soft shadows, and friendly icons.

MAIN WEBSITE STRUCTURE:

1. LANDING PAGE

Hero section with a **large full-width farm landscape image with tractors working in the field**.

Headline:
"Share Equipment. Grow Together."

Subtext:
"A local marketplace where farmers can rent tractors, harvesters, and agricultural tools from nearby owners."

Buttons:

* Find Equipment
* List Your Machine

Below the hero section include **equipment categories with image cards**:

🚜 Tractors
🌾 Harvesters
💧 Irrigation Systems
🌱 Seeders
🚛 Transport Equipment

Each category card should contain a large photo of the equipment in action.

2. EQUIPMENT DISCOVERY PAGE

Create a layout inspired by **Airbnb-style listings**.

Left side:
Scrollable equipment cards.

Right side:
Interactive map showing nearby machinery.

Equipment cards must include:

* large equipment photo
* equipment name
* distance from user
* price per hour
* availability indicator

Example card content:
Mahindra Tractor
3 km away
₹550 per hour
Available today

Cards should highlight imagery and feel tactile and friendly.

3. EQUIPMENT DETAIL PAGE

Large image gallery of the machine.

Include sections for:

* equipment photos
* description
* specifications
* price
* owner profile
* booking calendar

Include a **visual booking calendar** showing available dates.

Primary button:
"Request Booking"

4. LIST EQUIPMENT PAGE

Simple step-by-step form where owners can upload their machine.

Fields include:

* equipment name
* machine type
* photos
* location
* price per hour
* availability calendar

Include drag-and-drop image upload with preview thumbnails.

5. USER DASHBOARD

Design a **visual activity dashboard**, not a corporate analytics panel.

Sections include:

* My Equipment
* Upcoming Rentals
* Booking Requests
* Earnings Summary

Show equipment thumbnails and friendly widgets such as:

Your Tractor
Booked 4 times this week

Total Earnings
₹8,200

6. MAP VIEW

Include a **full-screen map showing available equipment nearby**.

Use tractor icons instead of standard location pins.

Clicking an icon opens a mini card showing:

* equipment photo
* price
* quick book button

INTERACTIONS & MICRO-ANIMATIONS:

Equipment cards slightly lift on hover.

Tractor icons animate subtly on the map.

When a booking succeeds, show a small tractor icon animation driving across a field.

MOBILE DESIGN:

Ensure the UI works well on mobile devices with:

* large touch-friendly buttons
* swipeable equipment cards
* simple navigation
* fast search

TONE:

The platform should feel like a **community tool built for real farmers**, not a corporate software product.

Prioritize imagery, simplicity, and local marketplace vibes.

Avoid:
enterprise UI
pricing pages
corporate stock photography
complex enterprise dashboards
